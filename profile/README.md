## 🧠 What is Conform Object?  

The **Conform Object** addon is a powerful tool for Blender that allows users to align and conform objects to different surfaces, geometries, or meshes with precision. Whether you’re working with complex models or trying to match objects to specific curves, this addon makes it simple to align them perfectly without manual adjustments.

Perfect for modelers and animators, Conform Object streamlines the process of conforming objects, saving valuable time when positioning and adjusting assets in 3D scenes. With customizable settings, you can easily tweak how the objects conform to surfaces, allowing for flexible and creative adjustments.

Fully compatible with Blender on Windows, Conform Object is an essential addition to your 3D modeling toolkit, helping you maintain efficiency and accuracy when working with object alignments.
